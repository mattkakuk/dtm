## Hyper-Local Review Pages

 Quick check-ins every time you run an appointment will greatly increase your online visibility. These check-ins will push your location and keywords to all the major search engines.  
  
After you check-in, you can invite your customers to leave a review for your business, which will automatically show up on your website tagging the geo-coordinates with it. This system is preferred by Google, so the review pages help you get ranked for your top keywords in the cities they are created in.  
  
Since the review is an invitation-only, you do not have to worry about an ex-employee or rouge competition leaving negative ones.
